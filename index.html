Butterfly Effect

Unreal Engine Project

Butterfly Effect is a VR game designed to deliver an immersive experience by minimizing distractions such as clunky VR menus. Instead, it focuses on intuitive, natural hand interactions, allowing players to seamlessly engage with their surroundings. The objective is to creatively set off chain reactions within each level, making every interaction meaningful and captivating.
